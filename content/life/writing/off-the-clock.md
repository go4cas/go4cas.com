The best thing I ever did for my work was to take the parts of my life that have
nothing to do with it seriously.

## Two worlds, one person

There's a temptation to let one identity eat the rest — to become only the
builder, only the job. But the outdoors, the gym, the music turned up too loud
in the garage: those aren't the opposite of the work. They're what keeps the
person doing the work intact.

## The handoff

Some days the ideas that unstick a hard problem don't arrive at the desk. They
arrive on a trail, or under a bar, or somewhere with no screen in sight. You
can't schedule that. You can only make room for it.

Drink beer. Eat meat. Lift heavy.
